## About the Role
Azure Security is looking for a TPM to lead initiatives in identity and access management (IAM). You will work on global-scale services that protect millions of users.

## Responsibilities
- Partner with engineering to deliver secure, scalable identity solutions.
- Drive the roadmap for cross-tenant authentication and authorization features.
- Define success metrics and track progress against security compliance goals.
- Engage with customers to gather requirements for hybrid identity scenarios.

## Qualifications
- 5+ years of experience in technical program management or product management.
- Deep understanding of identity protocols (OAuth2, OIDC, SAML).
- Strong project management skills and experience with Agile methodologies.
- Passion for security and protecting user data.
